Children's Privacy & Families Policy

Child-Directed Treatment

  • Target Age: 3-6 years (preschool)
  • Parental Consent: Required before collecting any child data
  • Data Minimization: Only necessary information collected
  • Ad Treatment: Child-directed treatment enabled (COPPA compliant)
  • Ad Rating: G (General Audiences) maximum
  • No Personalized Ads: For children
  • No Precise Location: Only approximate via IP for security
  • Parental Rights: Review, delete, or correct child's data
