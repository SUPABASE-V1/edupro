1. Introduction

Welcome to EduDash Pro, an AI-powered educational platform designed specifically for South African preschools. We are committed to protecting your privacy and the privacy of the children using our platform.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, share, and protect your personal information in compliance with:

  • Protection of Personal Information Act (POPIA), 2013 - South African data protection law
  • General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) - European data protection standards
  • Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) - US children's privacy law
  • Google Play Families Policy - Requirements for apps targeting children

2. Scope and Application

2.1 Who We Are

EduDash Pro (Pty) Ltd is a South African company that provides a multi-tenant Software-as-a-Service (SaaS) platform for preschool education. We serve four primary user roles:

  • Principals - School administrators managing their preschool
  • Teachers - Educators creating lessons and tracking student progress
  • Parents - Guardians accessing their children's educational information
  • Students - Preschool children (ages 3-6) with age-appropriate profiles

2.2 Multi-Tenant Architecture

Our platform uses strict data isolation. Each preschool (tenant) has completely separate data that cannot be accessed by other preschools. All data queries are filtered by preschool_id with Row-Level Security (RLS) policies enforced at the database level.

8. Data Security

8.1 Encryption

  • In Transit: All data transmitted using TLS/HTTPS encryption
  • At Rest: Database encryption managed by Supabase (AES-256)
  • Passwords: Hashed using bcrypt with salt
  • Biometric Data: Stored locally on device only (Expo SecureStore)

8.2 Access Controls

  • Role-Based Access Control (RBAC): superadmin, principal, teacher, parent roles
  • Row-Level Security (RLS): Database policies enforce tenant isolation
  • Multi-Factor Authentication: Optional for enhanced security
  • Session Management: Secure tokens with automatic expiration

8.3 Infrastructure Security

  • Multi-tenant architecture with strict data isolation by preschool_id
  • Regular security audits and penetration testing
  • Rate limiting to prevent abuse and DDoS attacks
  • Security event logging for audit trails
  • Service role operations restricted to server-side only

8.4 Breach Notification

In the event of a data breach:

  • We will notify affected users within 72 hours (GDPR/POPIA requirement)
  • We will notify relevant data protection authorities
  • We will provide details about the breach and mitigation steps
