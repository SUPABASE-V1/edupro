### Color System:
- **Primary**: Blue for main actions
- **Success**: Green for good scores (>70%)
- **Warning**: Orange for medium scores (50-70%)
- **Danger**: Red for low scores (<50%)
- **Muted**: Gray for secondary text

### Spacing System:
- Section margins: `var(--space-4)`
- Card padding: `var(--space-3)` or `var(--space-4)`
- Grid gaps: `var(--space-3)`
- Button padding: `var(--space-3)`
